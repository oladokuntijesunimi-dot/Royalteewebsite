Our Story

Where Heritage Meets the Needle

Royal Tee Stitches began as a single sewing table in Bodija, Ibadan, built on a simple promise: every garment should fit like it was made for one person — because it was. Today, we marry traditional Nigerian textile artistry with contemporary tailoring techniques, crafting Ankara, Aso Ebi, Agbada and corporate wear for clients who refuse to compromise on fit or finish.

Every order travels through the same disciplined process: precise measurement, hand-selected fabric, and a final fitting overseen by our master tailors before it ever leaves the atelier.
